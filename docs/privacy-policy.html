Privacy Policy

P2PGames for Android · Effective date: 8 July 2026

In short: P2PGames collects no data. The app has no internet access requirement for gameplay, no accounts, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. Everything you enter stays on your device or travels only directly to the devices you play with over Wi-Fi Direct.

Who we are

P2PGames is a local multiplayer game app for Android published by Nico Christmann ("we", "us"). For any privacy question, contact us at nicochristmann3@gmail.com.

What data the app processes

The app processes the following information, all of it solely on your device and on the devices of the people you play with:

  • Display name — the name you type on the home screen. It is sent over the local Wi-Fi Direct connection to the other players in your session so they can see who they are playing with. It is not stored after you close the app and never leaves the local connection.
  • Game state — moves, cards, dice rolls and similar gameplay data are exchanged directly between the connected devices for the duration of a session and are not stored.
  • Device names — while searching for a session, the app shows the Wi-Fi Direct device names of nearby devices, as provided by Android. The app does not record them.

What we do not do

  • No collection, storage or transmission of personal data to us or to anyone else — the app contains no server backend of ours; game traffic is exclusively peer-to-peer between your devices.
  • No accounts or sign-in.
  • No advertising, no analytics, no crash-reporting SDKs, no tracking of any kind.
  • No access to your contacts, files, camera, microphone or precise location for our own purposes (see the permissions note below).
  • No sale or sharing of data — we have nothing to sell or share.

About the permissions the app requests

  • Nearby Wi-Fi devices (Android 13 and newer) — required by Android to discover and connect to nearby players over Wi-Fi Direct. The app declares to Android that this permission is never used to derive your location.
  • Location (Android 12 and older only) — on these Android versions the system requires the location permission for Wi-Fi Direct peer discovery. The app does not read, compute, store or transmit your location; the permission is used exclusively so that Android allows the device scan.
  • Wi-Fi control permissions — used to create and join the local Wi-Fi Direct connection that the games run over.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences. Since no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children either.

Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability and objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Because we do not collect or store any personal data, there is typically nothing we could look up, hand over or delete — but you can always reach us at the address above with any request or question.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes what data is processed (for example, by adding an online mode), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change.
